Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register as an artisan on SkillTube?

To register as an artisan, click on the "Add Your Work" button on our homepage and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide some basic information about yourself and your craft, along with a verification of your craftsmanship.

How does the payment system work for artisans?

Once you upload your work and set prices, customers can place orders directly. We handle the payment processing and release funds to your registered bank account within 5-7 business days after order fulfillment. We charge a small platform fee of 8% per transaction.

Can I ship my products internationally?

Yes! SkillTube supports international shipping. When you list your products, you can specify your shipping preferences. We have partnered with several logistics providers to ensure your crafts reach customers worldwide safely.

Does SkillTube offer any training for artisans?

Yes, we regularly organize digital workshops on product photography, pricing strategies, and online marketing specifically tailored for traditional artisans. Check our "Artisan Resources" section for upcoming workshops.
