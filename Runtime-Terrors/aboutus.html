Our Mission

Preserving traditions, empowering artisans, connecting communities

Our Story

SkillTube was born from a simple observation: India's rich artistic heritage was at risk of being lost in the digital age. Traditional craftspeople with decades of experience were struggling to find markets for their products, while younger generations were leaving these crafts behind.

Founded in 2023, our platform began as a small project to document the techniques of master craftsmen in a few villages. Today, we've grown into India's largest digital platform connecting skilled artisans directly with appreciative customers worldwide.

Our team consists of technology experts, cultural preservationists, and business professionals – all united by our passion for India's artistic heritage and the belief that traditional crafts can thrive in the modern economy.

Our Values

Authenticity
